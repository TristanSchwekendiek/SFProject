Our History

Since its inception in 1987, Street Fighter has been synonymous with arcade-style fighting games, with its classic characters, thrilling battles, and intense competition. From Ryu's powerful Hadouken to Chun-Li's lightning-fast kicks, the Street Fighter universe is full of memorable moments that have become embedded in gaming culture.

At Capcom, our goal is to create games that embody the spirit of competition and the thrill of victory. We're constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in fighting games, with innovative gameplay features, stunning graphics, and immersive sound effects that transport players to the heart of the action.

Our team of talented developers, designers, and artists is passionate about creating games that deliver unforgettable experiences for players of all ages and skill levels. We're constantly exploring new ideas and technologies, collaborating with partners around the world, and engaging with our vibrant community of players to create the best possible games.

We're proud of our legacy as one of the most iconic and beloved franchises in gaming history, and we're committed to continuing to deliver top-quality games that push the genre forward and keep players coming back for more.
